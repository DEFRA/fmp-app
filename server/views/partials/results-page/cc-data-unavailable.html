Climate change data unavailable

In this location, all or part of the site is included in an area where flood zones plus climate change data is not currently available while we make important improvements to our data.

For development sites falling within these locations, further assessment is needed to inform:

  • the application of the sequential and exception tests
  • the need for a flood risk assessment (FRA)

The Environment Agency may still hold other flood risk information that can be used to inform a flood risk assessment. Strategic flood risk assessments should also be checked for relevant information in these locations.
