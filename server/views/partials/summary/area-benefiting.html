Flood zone 3

area that benefits from flood defences

Planning authorities regard these areas as those likely to flood if nearby flood defences were not present

More information about flood zones
  1. You may need to do a flood risk assessment for development in an area benefiting from flood defences.
  2. Find out the level of flood protection offered at your development's location. You can do this by talking to your local Partnerships and Strategic Overview team. You'll also need to obtain a Flood Defence Breach Hazard Map (this is also known as a 'Product 8' by planning authorities).
  3. For some kinds of flood zone 3 development you must follow the Environment Agency standing advice for doing a flood risk assessment.
  4. You can also read more about flood risk assessments for planning applications.
