Decide what you need for your planning application

Your development has a site area of {{plotSize}} hectares (ha).

You only need a flood risk assessment when a development in flood zone 1:

  • has a site area of 1 ha or more
  • is in an area with critical drainage problems
  • where the local planning authority's (LPA) strategic flood risk assessment (SFRA) shows it will be at increased flood risk in future
  • is at risk from other sources of flooding and its development would increase the vulnerability of its use

Contact your local planning authority, {{localAuthorities}}, to check its SRFA.
