How likely a flood is
The chance of surface water (or flash) flooding at this location is more than 3.3% each year.
How deep a flood could be
If this location flooded, the water would most likely be less than 30cm deep.

How likely a flood is
There is a risk of flooding from reservoirs in this location. If you are considering development in an area of reservoir flood risk, you should contact the reservoir owner(s) to help you understand:
  • the flood risk in more detail
  • if the development could affect the reservoir’s risk designation, it’s design category or how it is operated

Next steps for your planning application

Decide what you need for your planning application

Contact York local planning authority to check their planning requirements.

Get a Flood Risk Assessment (FRA). You will normally need to pay a flood risk specialist to carry it out.

For developments is flood zone 3 your FRA should include and assessment of all sources of flooding and take into account climate change allowances.

Find out how to do an assessment.
