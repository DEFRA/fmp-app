To request flood risk assessment data (previously known as a product 4) you’ll need an email address we can send the information to.

  • historic flood outlines
  • relevant modelled flood levels and extents
  • flood defence locations and attributes

You will need to do a flood risk assessment if your development is any of the following:

  • bigger than 1 hectare (ha)
  • in an area with critical drainage problems as notified by the Environment Agency
  • in an area that could be affected by sources of flooding other than rivers and the sea (such as surface water or reservoirs)
  • a change of use in development type to a more vulnerable category (for example from commercial to residential) and in an area that could be affected by sources of flooding other than rivers and the sea

If your development doesn't fall into any of these categories, you only need to download the flood zone map on this page to include in your planning application.

You can still use this service to request flood risk data (previously known as a product 4). However, as your site is in flood zone 1, it’s unlikely there will be any data available.
