Flood map for planning

This service is for learning about a site location's flood probability as part of development planning.

Before you start

This service has 3 steps:

  1. Provide a postcode, place or National Grid Reference (NGR) where your development will take place
  2. Confirm the location using a map
  3. View a map showing the probability of flooding in the area

Enter your development's location to view the probability of it flooding
