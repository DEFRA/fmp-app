Find out if you need a Flood Risk Assessment (FRA) and get flood maps for a planning application

Use this service to:

  • find out if you need an FRA as part of a planning application (sometimes known as planning permission)
  • download or request the map products you need for an FRA
How this service works

Whether you need to do an FRA depends on what flood zone the development is in. There are 3 flood zones, 1, 2 and 3.

All developments in flood zones 2 and 3 will need an FRA.

Some developments in flood zone 1 will need an FRA.

We will tell you what flood zone the development is in and how to get the map products you need for your application.

You’ll usually need a flood risk specialist to carry out the FRA. If it’s for a simple, low risk development like a house extension you may be able to do it yourself.

If you’re unsure contact the Environment Agency.

Your planning application may be refused by your local council if you don’t include an FRA or if it isn’t satisfactory

Read detailed guidance on Flood Risk Assessments.

Before you start

To select an area, you will need either a:

  • Postcode
  • Place name
  • National Grid Reference (NGR)
  • Easting and northing co-ordinates
