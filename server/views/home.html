Get flood risk information for planning

Use this service to download flood risk data (previously known as a product 4) without having to contact the Environment Agency. This service replaces flood maps for planning.

Use this service to:

  • find out if you need a flood risk assessment as part of a planning application (sometimes known as planning permission)
  • get flood risk assessment data
Start now

Before you start

You’ll usually need a flood risk consultant to carry out a flood risk assessment. If it’s for a simple, low risk development like a house extension you may be able to do it yourself.

If you’re unsure contact the Environment Agency.

Find out more about flood risk assessments for planning permission.

For general flood information, including local flood warnings, use the flood information service.
