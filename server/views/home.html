Development planning and flood risk

This service is for learning about a site location's flood risk as part of development planning.

For general flood information, including local flood warnings, use the flood information service

Before you start

This service has 3 steps:

  1. Provide a postcode or National Grid Reference (NGR) where your development will take place
  2. Confirm the location using a map
  3. View a map showing the likelihood of flooding in the area

Enter your development's location to get an assessment of its flood risk
