Help with flood maps for planning

Whether you need to do an FRA depends on what flood zone the development is in. There are 3 flood zones, 1, 2 and 3.

All developments in flood zones 2 and 3 will need an FRA. Some developments in flood zone 1 will need an FRA.

View more detailed guidance about flood zones FRA requirements

You will need an FRA in flood zone 1 if the development is:

  • more than 1 hectare
  • in an area that has critical drainage problems notified by the Environment Agency

You will need an FRA in flood zone 1 for developments of less than 1 hectare and:

  • you change the use to a more vulnerable class (for example, from commercial to residential)
  • it could be affected by sources of flooding other than from rivers and the sea (for example surface water or reservoirs)

We will tell you what flood zone the development is in and the maps you will need for your planning application.

Before you start

For the location you want to check you will need either the:

  • postcode or place name
  • National Grid Reference (NGR)
  • easting or northing co-ordinates
  • shapefile

After you have found the location on the map you can confirm it with a marker or draw a site.
