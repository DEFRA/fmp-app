Flood zones explained

Flood zones determine how likely it is that a location will flood.

All locations in flood zones 2 and 3 need a flood risk assessment. Some locations in flood zone 1 need a flood risk assessment.

Flood zone Probability of flooding Assessment needed?
1 Low Some
2 Medium Yes
3 High Yes

Find out more about which developments need a flood risk assessment in flood zone 1.

Getting a flood risk assessment

You can use this service to download flood risk data that can be used as part of a flood risk assessment. However, some developments are more complex than others, and this data isn’t always enough to get planning permission.

We recommend that you work with a flood risk consultant to get your flood risk assessment.
