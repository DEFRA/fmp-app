How to draw a site boundary:

  1. Select ‘Start drawing’ at the top of the map.
  2. Work your way round your site, selecting each corner in turn.
  3. When you return to your starting point, double click.
  4. You can edit the shape by dragging the points or adding more.
  5. When you’re done, select ‘Continue’.

If your site covers more than one flood zone, the report will be for the highest zone in your site.
