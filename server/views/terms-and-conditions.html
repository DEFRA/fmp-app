Flood map for planning service

The flood map for planning service shows river and sea flood zones derived from modelled local and national river and sea data.

The flood zones don't include other sources of flooding and ignore the presence of defences.

This information provides an indication of the likelihood of flooding from rivers or the sea and is intended only for use in development planning and flood risk assessments.

All information is a broad indicator of an area’s flood risk. As such it is not suitable for identifying whether an individual property will flood. As our information relates to an area rather than a specific property, not all properties within that area (which could be a street or a postcode) will necessarily be at the same risk of flooding. This might be the case if the property is on higher land or the floor levels are high.

Flooding can be caused by other water sources, for example surface water, overwhelmed sewers or drains, or groundwater from underground aquifers.
